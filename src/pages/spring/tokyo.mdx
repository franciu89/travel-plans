# Tokyo Itinerary

Welcome to the Tokyo itinerary! Here, you'll find a detailed guide to exploring the vibrant city of Tokyo.

## Highlights

- **Shibuya Crossing**: Experience the world's busiest pedestrian crossing.
- **Tokyo Tower**: Enjoy breathtaking views of the city.
- **Asakusa**: Visit the historic Senso-ji Temple.
- **Akihabara**: Dive into the world of anime and electronics.

## Day 1: Arrival and Exploration

- Arrive in Tokyo and check into your accommodation.
- Explore the local neighborhood and try some authentic Japanese cuisine.

## Day 2: Cultural Landmarks

- Visit the Meiji Shrine and Yoyogi Park.
- Stroll through Harajuku and Omotesando for shopping and unique fashion.

## Day 3: Modern Tokyo

- Spend the day in Odaiba, a futuristic man-made island.
- Enjoy a ride on the Yurikamome Line for stunning views.
